Introduction to Labour Law and the Basic Conditions of Employment Act

Course Information

Understanding labour law is essential for ensuring fair, legal, and ethical workplace practices. This course provides a solid foundation in South African labour legislation, focusing on the rights and responsibilities of both employers and employees. Learners will explore the Basic Conditions of Employment Act (BCEA), key employment contracts, and the dynamics of individual and collective labour relations.
