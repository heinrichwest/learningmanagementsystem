Using Statistics and Probability to Analyse and Communicate Life Problems

Course Information

This course provides an in-depth exploration of statistical methods and probability concepts applied to real-world problems, equipping students with the knowledge and skills to critically interrogate, analyse, and communicate statistical findings in various life contexts. Through structured units and sub-units, learners will be guided through the processes of identifying relevant situations, gathering and organising data, applying appropriate statistical methods, and drawing valid conclusions based on sound reasoning. The course emphasizes both theoretical and practical aspects of data analysis, with a focus on ensuring that findings are accurate, reliable, and clearly communicated to diverse audiences.

Level: All Levels Duration: 3h 30m Mode: Online
