Functions and Features

Purpose-built tools to plan, manage, and report Employment Equity with confidence.

Core Functions

  • Workforce analysis by occupational level, category, race, and gender
  • Representation tracking vs. national and sectoral targets
  • Plan creation with annual targets, variances, and gap analysis
  • Barrier identification, affirmative action measures, and progress logs
  • Submission-ready reports and audit evidence generation
  • Role-based access control and activity audit trails

Key Outcomes

  • Clear visibility of current and target workforce profiles
  • Faster, accurate compliance submissions and reporting
  • Transparent governance with evidence-backed decisions
  • Traceable progress across planning cycles and audits
