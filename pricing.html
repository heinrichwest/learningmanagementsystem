Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our pricing and plans.

Can I change plans at any time?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and billing is prorated.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees for Starter and Professional plans. Enterprise plans may include implementation services with transparent pricing.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and purchase orders for Enterprise customers.

Do you offer discounts for educational institutions?

Yes, we offer special pricing for accredited educational institutions and non-profit organizations. Contact sales for details.

What happens if I exceed my user limit?

We'll notify you when approaching your limit. You can upgrade your plan or purchase additional user licenses.

Is there a money-back guarantee?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for annual subscriptions. No questions asked.
