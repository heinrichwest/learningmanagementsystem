Finance for Non-Financial Managers

This course enables you to understand your company's financials. It addresses everything from basic accounting terminology and processes, budgeting and ethics, to understanding and working with the various accounting documents.

Level: Intermediate
Duration: 3 hours
Units: 7 units
Enroll Now Request Info

What You'll Learn

  • Interpret balance sheets and income statements
  • Make informed financial decisions
  • Apply basic accounting principles
