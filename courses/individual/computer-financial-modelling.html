Financial Modelling

While there is a lot of value in mastering the basic and intermediate array of functions offered by Microsoft Excel, for some, simply automating the day-to-day arithmetic functions involved in a simple balance sheet will not be enough to meet the financial accounting needs of a large company.

Level: Advanced
Duration: 3 hours
Units: 8 units
Enroll Now Request Info

What You'll Learn

  • Create automated reporting systems

  • Design cash flow and amortization models
