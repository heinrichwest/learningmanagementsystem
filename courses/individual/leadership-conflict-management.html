Conflict Management

Conflict in the workplace can be a normal part of doing business. When properly managed, it can be beneficial as it fosters an environment of healthy competition. However, conflict may also have a detrimental effect.

Level: Intermediate
Duration: 3 hours
Units: 4 units

  • Apply conflict resolution techniques

  • Create policies for conflict prevention
