Customer Satisfaction

Customer satisfaction is a measurement of how products and services supplied by a company meet or surpass customer expectations. With this online course, you will learn how to build customer loyalty, how to satisfy the nine essential needs of customers, and how your company's values, knowledge and attitude determines your behaviour.

Level: Basic
Duration: 3 hours
Units: 4 units
