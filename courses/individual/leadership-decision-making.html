Decision-Making

Decision-making is a key skill in any organisation and is especially important if you want to be an effective leader. Whether you are deciding which person to recruit, which supplier to use or which strategy to follow, the ability to make good decisions is fundamental.

Level: Intermediate
Duration: 3 hours
Units: 4 units

  • Engage stakeholders in decision-making

  • Communicate decisions effectively
