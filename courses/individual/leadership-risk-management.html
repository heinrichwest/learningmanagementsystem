Risk Management

This course starts by exploring the concept of risk and the factors that can constitute risks, i.e., legislation affecting business operations in South Africa, health risks and a few others. It looks at organisational policies and procedures as the strategic link between a company's vision and its day-to-day operations.

Level: Advanced
Duration: 3 hours
Units: 4 units
Enroll Now Request Info

What You'll Learn

  • Identify and assess business risks
  • Develop risk mitigation strategies
  • Create contingency planning processes
  • Implement risk management systems
