Building Teams

Many people tend to assume groups and teams are the same. In reality, the two are very different. In this course, you will look at the theory of teams and the role of teams in the workplace, the types and importance of teams, as well as the qualities of a good team.

Level: Intermediate
Duration: 3 hours
Units: 5 units
Enroll Now Request Info

What You'll Learn

  • Build and develop high-performing teams
  • Understand team dynamics and stages
  • Resolve conflicts within teams
  • Delegate effectively and empower team members

Course Outline

Unit 1: Theory of teams and its role in workplace

+
  • Theory of teams and its role in the workplace
  • Types of teams
  • Importance of teams
  • Qualities of a good team

Unit 2: Theory of teams and team dynamics

+
  • Theory of teams and team dynamics (Group norms, Presence of others)
  • Group conflicts
  • Stages of conflict

Unit 3: Team building

+
  • Team building
  • Team building process
  • Stages of team development
  • Storming
  • Norming
  • Promoting trust in the team
  • Promoting cohesion in the team
  • Promoting creativity in the team
  • Promoting productivity in the team by delegating

Unit 4: Team leaders

+
  • Team leadership
  • Leadership styles
  • Laissez-faire style of leadership
  • Bureaucratic leadership
  • Problem-solving in teams

Unit 5: Evaluating team effectiveness

+
  • Evaluating team effectiveness
  • Analysis of team effectiveness
  • Developing an action plan
  • Obtaining team commitment to the action plan
