Intermediate Microsoft Word

The intermediate course offers a more in-depth look at Microsoft Word and its functions. The course shows you how to work with tables and charts. You will also learn how to change the style of a document, and work with pictures and graphics.

Level: Intermediate
Duration: 3 hours
Units: 7 units
Enroll Now Request Info

What You'll Learn

  • Work with advanced tables and charts
  • Apply styles and themes for professional look
  • Use mail merge for bulk communications
  • Create templates and manage document versions
