Workplace Relations

In today's working environment, it is only individuals with excellent interpersonal skills who rise to the top in their personal effectiveness and organisational growth. This is because interpersonal skills enable employees to successfully navigate the dynamic and challenging workplace environment.

Level: Intermediate
Duration: 3 hours
Units: 4 units
